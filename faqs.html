Q1: Can I attend a demo session before enrollment?

We have a limited number of participants in a live session to maintain the Quality Standards. So, unfortunately, participation in a live class without enrollment is not possible. However, you can go through the sample class recordings, which would give you a clear insight into how the classes are conducted, the quality of instructors, and the level of interaction in a class.
